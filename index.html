How to use this dashboard

  • Hover charts to see tooltips with exact values.
  • Time chart: click a point to filter by year. Double-click to reset.
  • Breakdown mode: click legend items to highlight one outcome line.
  • Intake stacked bars: hover segments to see share and totals.
  • Small multiples: use the toggle to switch Adoption rate vs Median stay.
  • Map: use fullscreen for easier viewing.
Tip: Use the highlights to connect intake patterns with outcomes over time.
